Seed Information & Types

Learn about different types of seeds and their uses

Vegetable Seeds

Includes seeds like tomato, cucumber, spinach, and lettuce. Ideal for kitchen gardens and farms.

Flower Seeds

Common flower seeds include marigold, sunflower, rose, and petunia. Great for ornamental gardening.

Herb Seeds

Herbs like basil, mint, thyme, and coriander are popular for both culinary and medicinal use.

Fruit Seeds

Fruit seeds such as watermelon, papaya, and pomegranate are used in orchards and large gardens.
